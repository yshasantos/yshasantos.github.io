CASA at UofT

October 2022 - Present

The Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence Students Association (CASA) is a student-run organization at the University of Toronto that aims to foster a community of students interested in cognitive science.

President (2024-2025)

As the student organization's president, I am responsible for overseeing the executive team and ensuring that the organization's goals are met.

Some other responsiblities include:

  • Attending ASSU meetings as a member of the council
  • Leading general meetings for the CASA student body
  • Attending program meetings as a student representative
  • Leading Executive Meetings and alocating tasks
  • Supervising executive member's progress on their tasks
  • Hosting office hours to help fellow students

Communications Director (2023-2024)

As the communications director, I was responsible for creating advertising designs for events, managing the organization's social media accounts, and sending out newsletters to members.

Some other responsiblities include:

  • Responding to queries about CASA or the cognitive science program
  • Ensuring easy access of resources for students
  • Hosting office hours to help fellow students

COG250 Representative (2022-2023)

Some other responsiblities include:
