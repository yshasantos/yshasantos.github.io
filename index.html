Hey! I'm Patricia

I'm a fourth year student at the University of Toronto studying Cognitive Science, Computer Science, and Psychology. I'd consider myself a creative and analytical thinker who enjoys making things that will help others!

My interdisciplinary field of study allows me to understand the intersection of human behavior and technology, which I hope to showcase in my work. I aim to apply these skills to UX design and front-end development through making optimized and easy to learn user interfaces.

Experience and Works

Blueprint Project Designer

Working with a team of developers, I am tasked with making designs which follow our client's design requirements. Currently, my team is collaborating with The Period Purse to improve their mobile period tracking app.

CASA President

Serving as the 2024-2025 President of the Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence Students Association (CASA), it is my job to manage the executive team and ensure that We are effectively providing resources and events for our members.

Linguistics Lab Research Assistant

As a lab assistant, I was responsible for organizing data for the lab's research on linguistic processing of bilingual children. I also helped design the experiment's interface, such that it would be easily comprehensible for children.

Hobby Art

In my free time, I enjoy creating digital art, and often take commissions from friends and family. I study the principles of design and color theory to create pieces that are visually appealing, which I hope to apply to my future work in UX design.

CSC318 @ UofT

In The Design of Interactive Computational Media, my group set out to create a mobile app prototype resembling a social media platform. My role included creating user personas, wireframes, and conducting usability testing.
