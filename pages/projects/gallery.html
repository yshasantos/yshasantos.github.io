> Wander UX Designathon '25

Project title: Infinite Gallery
Team: Design Enjoyers

March 21, 2025 - March 23, 2025

Infinite Gallery is conceptualized to be a web application that allows artist to upload and share their creations in an interactive gallery format. Inspired by the holitic feeling of walking through an art market and discovering new artists alongside others, this app aims to create a sense of community with features such as voice notes, cursor sharing, voice chat, and theme-based algorithms.
