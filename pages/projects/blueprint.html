> Blueprint Product Designer

Team: The Period Purse 2024-2025

October 2024 - Present

For this role, I collaborated with a team of developers to improve the design of The Period Purse's period tracking app, Menustration Nation. I was tasked with creating the design and style guide for the new ovulation feature, and designing information pages for 6 different types of period products.

Ovulation Design

  • Conducted research on ovulation tracking and the ovulation cycle
  • Created wireframes and low fidelity prototypes for ovulation tracking, along with icon drafts
  • Created the final icon and mockup for how the ovulation feature should look

Period Product Info Pages Design

  • Conducted research on the 6 different types of period products to ensure the information provided was accurate
  • Created wireframes and low fidelity prototypes for the information pages
  • Created the final mockups for how the information pages should look like
